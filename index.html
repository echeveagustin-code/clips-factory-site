clips_factory

clips_factory is an automated system that processes long-form videos, detects short viral moments, converts them into vertical format, and uploads them to social media platforms.

How it works

The system downloads source videos, transcribes them, detects engaging moments, creates short clips, reformats them for vertical viewing, and uploads them through authorized platform accounts.
