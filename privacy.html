Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Clips Factory collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users access the Clips Factory website, connect a TikTok account through TikTok Login Kit, and use the content posting workflow made available through Clips Factory.

1. About Clips Factory

Clips Factory is a creator-facing tool and workflow that helps users connect their TikTok account, authenticate through TikTok Login, and continue a content-posting workflow through the Clips Factory website and related internal systems.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Clips Factory, we may collect and process the following information:

  • basic account information made available through authorized TikTok scopes;
  • authentication-related information such as authorization codes, access tokens, refresh tokens, and related connection metadata;
  • technical data such as browser type, IP address, device information, timestamps, and diagnostic logs;
  • website interaction data related to login, callback handling, dashboard access, and publishing workflow usage;
  • content workflow information, such as selected clips, upload status, or posting-related metadata.

3. How We Use Information

Clips Factory may use collected information to:

  • authenticate users and enable account connection through TikTok Login Kit;
  • support content posting and related workflow functionality;
  • operate, maintain, monitor, and improve the Clips Factory service;
  • troubleshoot technical problems, prevent abuse, and improve reliability;
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. TikTok Data and Permissions

When a user connects a TikTok account to Clips Factory, the user authorizes Clips Factory to access only the information and functionality permitted by the scopes granted by the user and approved by TikTok. Clips Factory does not claim ownership over a user’s TikTok account or content and only uses authorized access for the operation of the service.

5. Storage and Security

Clips Factory uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect connection data, tokens, logs, and related service data from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Sharing of Information

Clips Factory does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary to operate the service, comply with law, protect rights and security, or work with service providers that support hosting, storage, logging, infrastructure, or functionality related to Clips Factory.

7. Data Retention

Clips Factory retains information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, maintain account connection, support diagnostics, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements. Data may be deleted, anonymized, or removed when no longer necessary.

8. User Choices

Users may stop using Clips Factory at any time. Users may also disconnect their TikTok account or request deletion of relevant connection data, subject to any legal or operational retention requirements.

9. Children’s Privacy

Clips Factory is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use the service. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

10. International Use

If users access Clips Factory from outside the country where the service is operated, they understand that information may be processed and stored in other jurisdictions where data protection laws may differ.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Clips Factory may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of Clips Factory after an update may constitute acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
